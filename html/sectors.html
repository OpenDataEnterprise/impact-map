Open Data Use Across Sectors

Open data’s use spans all sectors of the economy: The Open Data Impact Map shows organizations utilizing this resource across 13 sectors. Each page below describes trends in use and the most used types of open data in each sector. Each page includes also use cases, a fact sheet and the data for the sector. The sectors with the most number of organizations are IT and geospatial, governance, and business, research and consulting.
