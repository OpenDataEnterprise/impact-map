Machine-Readability Project
The following use cases were developed as part of the ‘Open Data Impact Map - Machine Readability Project’. This project was conducted by the Center for Open Data Enterprise in collaboration with Open Data Watch. It was supported by the World Bank, with funding from the Trust Fund for Statistical Capacity Building. They represent examples of open data use in low to lower-middle income countries that showed evidence of use machine-readable open data. To download the data collected for the project, click here.
